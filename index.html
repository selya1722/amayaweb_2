ABOUT ME

Hello! I'm Amaya Jayasekara, an undergraduate at the Faculty of Technology, University of Sri Jayewardenepura.
I'm passionate about creating digital experiences, from designing websites, logos, and app interfaces to crafting engaging content for social media.

With hands-on experience in tools like Canva, Figma, Wix, Picsart, and CapCut, I'm currently expanding my skill set with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. Alongside my design work, I'm building my skills to become a proficient front-end developer,
